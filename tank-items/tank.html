Tank Items


Armor Items

Armor icon Armor, or AR, is one of the two damage resistance subtype stats. It reduces incoming physical damage by a percentage.

Magic Resist Items

Magic resistance icon Magic Resistance (also Magic Resist), or MR, is one of the two damage resistance subtype stats. It reduces incoming magic damage by a percentage.

Health Items

Health icon Health, or HP is a stat that determines the number of hit-points that a unit has. Damage reduces a unit's health, and Death occurs when a unit has taken enough damage to reach 0 health. Health can be restored via health regeneration, healing, life steal, and vamp.
